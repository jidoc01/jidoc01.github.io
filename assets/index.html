To use the fonts, add the following HTML code to your website:

<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="//undertalefonts.duodecima.technology/webfonts/stylesheet.css">

Then, you'll be able to use the fonts "Determination Mono", "Undertale Sans", and "Undertale Papyrus" on your website.

The fonts look best when your font size is a multiple of 16 pixels.

(You can also download the fonts and upload them to your own website. See the GitLab page for details.
